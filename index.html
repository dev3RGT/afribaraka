About Afribaraka

A proudly South African, Black-owned company providing integrated solutions across communications, media, mining support, construction and general supplies.

Established in 2016, Afribaraka has built a reputation for quality service and innovative, client-focused solutions for government institutions, municipalities, mining companies, private businesses and community organisations.

Driven by excellence and professionalism, we're committed to supporting economic growth and infrastructure development through reliable, cost-effective solutions tailored to every client's needs — delivered with the highest standards of quality, safety and integrity.

Vision
To become one of South Africa's leading integrated service providers in communications, mining support, construction and general supplies — through innovation, professionalism and service excellence.
Mission
Deliver high-quality, professional services that consistently exceed expectations, build sustainable partnerships on trust and accountability, and contribute to infrastructure development and community empowerment.
